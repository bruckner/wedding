Travel

Our wedding is in Berkeley, CA.

Berkeley

If you're flying, we recommend flights to SFO or OAK. The Oakland airport is a little closer to Berkeley, but it handles fewer airlines than SFO.

Much of the Bay Area—including Berkeley, San Francsico, and both airports above—is well served by public transit, namely BART, the subway. The Downtown Berkeley BART station is a block from Hotel Shattuck.

We will provide a shuttle service between Hotel Shattuck and the wedding venue on the evening of the ceremony, so you can get away without a rental car.

Travel to California on horseback is traditional, but not recommended.
